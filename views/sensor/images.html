Adding a weather or aurora camera

If you wish to add your own cam without using the Auroras.live Weathercam software, then you can do so easily

Requirements

In order to successfully upload an image to the sensor network, it must meet the following criteria:

  • The image must be greater than 800 pixels on the longest side
  • The image must be a JPEG
  • The image must contain the "Powered by Auroras.live" watermark
  • Your camera must be available at a set of reasonable hours. For weathercams, this could be dawn until dusk. For aurora cameras, dusk until dawn. High availability means a better end-user experience

Uploading your image

Using your method of choice (node.js and request + formdata work well), POST your image to

https://api.auroras.live/v1/integration/weathercam.php?key=yourapikeyhere
